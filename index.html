Marketing automation for K-HACCP — Korean food-safety HACCP solution

About

K-HACCP Marketing Automation is an internal tool that helps operators of K-HACCP, a Korean food-safety HACCP solution serving 200+ B2B customers, automatically generate and publish marketing content (short videos, blog posts, card news) to the operator's own social-media accounts including TikTok.

TikTok integration

  1. Login — operator authenticates with their own TikTok account via official OAuth 2.0.
  2. Draft — system generates short-form video drafts (caption + hashtags + clip) from K-HACCP product news, customer FAQs, and food-safety law updates.
  3. Review & Publish — operator reviews the draft and clicks Publish; the app calls video.publish on the operator's own account only.
  4. Dashboard — operator sees publish history and stats for their own posts only.

Scopes used: user.info.basic, video.upload, video.publish
Frequency: 1-3 posts/day on the operator's own account.
Audience: K-HACCP solution operators (B2B). End TikTok users see only the operator's published posts.

Compliance

  • All content is operator-created and operator-approved.
  • The app does NOT post third-party content, perform automated likes/follows/comments, mass-publish, or scrape other users.
  • Full adherence to TikTok Developer Terms and Community Guidelines.
